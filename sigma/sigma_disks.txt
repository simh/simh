Notes for the Sigma Disk Controllers

Two patterns of disk controllers - "10 byte" sense controllers and "16 byte" sense controllers
All disks have 1024B (256W) sectors

TIO, SIO, HIO status

	all

0	interrupt pending
1-2	device state
	00 = ready
	01 = not operational
	10 = reserved
	11 = busy
3	1 (automatic)
4	unusual end
5-6	controller state
	00 = ready
	01,10 = n/a
	11 = busy
7	0
